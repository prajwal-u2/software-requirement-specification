Requirements

  1. The system shall provide login functionality based on role
  2. The system shall manage various leagues through the use of a database
  3. The system shall have alerts regarding weather
  4. The system shall support different types of sports leagues and their respective tiers
  5. The system shall allow for league registration through an online payment system.

Specifications

  • The database governing the system as a whole will be postgres
  • Alters for weather related notifications will be pushed asynchronously without page refresh.
  • Various leagues will be viewable on a paginated screen witht he ability to sort based on league tier and/or sport
  • Payment systems will be conducted through stripe.
  • Login authentication will utilize a random salt and the md5 encryption algorithm
  • Authentication will support session management and JWT for remote access
